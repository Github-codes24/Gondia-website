Master of Business Administration (MBA)

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) program, affiliated with YCMOU, Nashik, is designed for graduates seeking flexible, weekend-based learning. Tailored for working professionals, business owners, and homemakers, the program offers expert faculty, industry guest lecturers, and practical exposure through industrial tours and internships. With a focus on soft skills, communication development, and subject-wise study materials, the MBA equips students for career growth. Job placement support further ensures real-world opportunities for all participants.

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program is a three-year, full-time course designed to nurture future professionals through experiential learning. At Shankarlal Agrawal College of Management Studies, the emphasis is on self-learning, with faculty acting as facilitators. The curriculum incorporates case studies, projects, industrial visits, and seminars to ensure practical, industry-oriented education. This holistic approach prepares students for real-world challenges beyond the classroom.
